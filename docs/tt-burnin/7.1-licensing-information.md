However, making, using, or selling hardware, models, or IP may require the license of rights (such as patent rights) from Tenstorrent or others.

While the Apache 2.0 license covers the software itself, users should be aware that:

*   **Software Usage**: The license grants rights to use, modify, and distribute the tt-burnin software
*   **Hardware Programming**: The software is designed to program Tenstorrent AI hardware products
*   **Hardware Rights**: Separate licensing may be required for manufacturing, using, or selling Tenstorrent hardware, models, or intellectual property
*   **Patent Rights**: Patent licenses from Tenstorrent or third parties may be necessary for hardware-related activities
