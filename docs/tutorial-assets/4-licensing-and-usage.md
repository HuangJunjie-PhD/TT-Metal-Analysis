## Compliance and Best Practices

### Documentation Integration Compliance

When integrating assets into documentation systems, follow these compliance patterns:

1.   **Direct Integration**: When assets are used directly in documentation, include attribution in image captions or nearby text
2.   **Modified Assets**: When assets are edited or adapted, clearly indicate modifications and retain original attribution
3.   **Aggregated Use**: When multiple assets are used together, provide comprehensive attribution listing

### Attribution Templates

For consistent attribution across different usage contexts:

**Basic Attribution Template:**

```
Image courtesy of Tenstorrent AI ULC. Copyright 2025. Licensed under CC BY 4.0.
Source: https://github.com/tenstorrent/tutorial-assets
```

**Modified Asset Attribution Template:**

Modifications: [description of changes]
Source: https://github.com/tenstorrent/tutorial-assets

## Downstream Usage and Distribution

The license terms ensure that downstream users receive the same rights while maintaining attribution requirements through the distribution chain.

### Distribution Chain Compliance

**Sources:**[LICENSE 111-124](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tutorial-assets/blob/eef92772/LICENSE#L111-L124)[README.md 8-16](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tutorial-assets/blob/eef92772/README.md?plain=1#L8-L16)

### Rights and Obligations Flow

The license creates a clear flow of rights and obligations that apply to all downstream users:

*   **Automatic License Grant**: All recipients automatically receive license rights
*   **Attribution Preservation**: Attribution requirements pass through to all downstream users
*   **No Additional Restrictions**: Downstream distributors cannot impose additional restrictions
*   **License Maintenance**: The CC BY 4.0 license terms remain in effect for all recipients

This licensing structure ensures that the image assets remain freely available for documentation and educational purposes while maintaining proper attribution to Tenstorrent AI ULC throughout the distribution chain.
