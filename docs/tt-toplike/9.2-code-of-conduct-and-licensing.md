This page details the community standards, legal licensing terms, and enforcement protocols for the `tt-toplike` project. It covers the project's adherence to the Contributor Covenant, the dual-licensing structure for code and documentation, and the specific reporting channels for community management.

## Code of Conduct

The `tt-toplike` community follows the **Contributor Covenant (version 2.1)** to ensure a harassment-free experience for all participants [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 1-5](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L1-L5) This policy applies to all community spaces and official representations of the project [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 33-35](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L33-L35)

### Enforcement Tiers

Community leaders are responsible for maintaining standards and may take corrective action ranging from warnings to permanent bans [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 27-31](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L27-L31) The project utilizes a four-tier enforcement ladder inspired by Mozilla’s guidelines:

| Tier | Impact Level | Consequence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **1. Correction** | Unprofessional language or unwelcome behavior [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 47-49](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L47-L49) | Private written warning and potential request for public apology [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 51-52](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L51-L52) |
| **2. Warning** | Single incident or series of actions [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 53-55](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L53-L55) | Period of restricted interaction; violation may lead to temporary/permanent ban [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 57-58](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L57-L58) |
| **3. Temporary Ban** | Serious violation or sustained inappropriate behavior [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 59-61](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L59-L61) | Complete ban from community interaction for a specified period [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 63-64](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L63-L64) |
| **4. Permanent Ban** | Pattern of violation, harassment, or disparagement of classes of individuals [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 65-67](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L65-L67) | Permanent ban from all public interaction within the community [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 69-70](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L69-L70) |

### Reporting Mechanism

Instances of unacceptable behavior should be reported to the Tenstorrent Open Source Program Office (OSPO) at **[ospo@tenstorrent.com](mailto:ospo@tenstorrent.com)**[CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 37-39](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L37-L39) All reports are investigated promptly, and leaders are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md 39-41](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md?plain=1#L39-L41)

## Licensing Framework

The project is governed by two primary licenses: the **Apache License 2.0** for software and the **Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International** for documentation and assets.

### Apache License 2.0 (Software)

The core codebase is licensed under the Apache License 2.0 [LICENSE 2-4](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/LICENSE#L2-L4) This provides users with a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, no-charge, and irrevocable copyright license to reproduce and distribute the work [LICENSE 67-72](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/LICENSE#L67-L72)

#### Key Provisions:

*   **Patent Grant:** Contributors grant a patent license for their contributions, which terminates if the user institutes patent litigation against any entity alleging the work constitutes infringement [LICENSE 74-88](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/LICENSE#L74-L88)
*   **Redistribution:** Redistribution is permitted provided that recipients receive a copy of the license, modified files carry prominent notices, and all attribution notices are retained [LICENSE 90-106](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/LICENSE#L90-L106)
*   **NOTICE File:** If the work includes a `NOTICE` file, its attribution notices must be included in any derivative works [LICENSE 107-116](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/LICENSE#L107-L116)
*   **Hardware Disclaimer:** For the avoidance of doubt, while the software is open source, making, using, or selling the hardware or IP associated with Tenstorrent products may require additional licenses (such as patent rights) [LICENSE_understanding.txt 1-4](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/LICENSE_understanding.txt#L1-L4)

### Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (Docs & Assets)

All documentation in the `docs/` directory and image files in the `assets/` directory are licensed under **CC-BY-4.0**[LICENSE-DOCS 1-8](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/LICENSE-DOCS#L1-L8) This allows for sharing and adapting the material for any purpose, even commercially, provided appropriate credit is given [LICENSE-DOCS 63-72](https://github.com/tenstorrent/tt-toplike/blob/487bfe5a/LICENSE-DOCS#L63-L72)
